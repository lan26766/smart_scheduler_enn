Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Smart Scheduler Pro

Can PlayScheduler replace our school bell system?

Yes, absolutely. PlayScheduler is widely used as an automated school bell system. You can schedule different bells for different periods, days, and even create special schedules for exams or events. The software provides precise timing and reliable operation.

How does the emergency broadcast feature work?

The emergency broadcast feature allows you to immediately interrupt any scheduled content with a priority message. You can trigger it manually from the software interface, via a hotkey, or remotely through network commands. After the emergency message plays, the system automatically returns to the scheduled content.

What media formats does the software support?

PlayScheduler supports all major media formats: Video (MP4, AVI, MOV, WMV), Audio (MP3, WAV, AAC, FLAC), Images (JPG, PNG, GIF), and live streams (RTMP, HLS). It also supports PowerPoint presentations and PDF files for digital signage applications.

Is internet connection required for the software to work?

No, once installed and activated, PlayScheduler works completely offline. All scheduling and playback is handled locally on your Windows PC. Internet is only required for software updates, live stream playback, or if you're using cloud-based content management.

What does the lifetime license include?

The $84 lifetime license includes: Permanent use of PlayScheduler on one computer, free minor updates (version 2.x), one year of premium support, and the ability to reinstall on the same computer. Major version upgrades (e.g., 3.0) may require an upgrade fee at a discounted rate.
